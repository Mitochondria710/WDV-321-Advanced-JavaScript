Ingredients:
  • 0.5 Cup Butter
  • 0.5 Cup Shortening
  • 0.25 Cup Sugar
  • 0.75 Cup Brown Sugar
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 0.5 tsp Burnt Sugar Flavor
  • 1 Box Instant Vanilla Pudding
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 1/2 Cups Flour
  • 1 tsp Baking Soda
  • 0.25 tsp salt
  • 1 Package Chocolate Chips
Instructions:
  • Cream together the butter, shortening, and sugars
  • Add the vanilla extract, burnt sugar flavor, pudding mix, and Eggs
  • Sift together the flour, baking soda and salt, and then add them to the previous mixture.
  • Add the chocolate chips.
  • Scoop into balls and bake on a cookie sheet at 375 degrees, for 10-12 minutes

Ingredients

  • 0.25 tsp Salt

  • 2 tsp Almond Extract
